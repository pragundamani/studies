## Basics
To make inline math like this $1^1+\sqrt{1}=2$ use `$` around math equation `$1^1+\sqrt{1}=2$`   
To make math blocks like this $$1^1+\sqrt{1}=2$$
use `$$` around math equation `$$1^1+\sqrt{1}=2$$`
